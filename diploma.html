About Department

Diploma in Islamic

This course is designed for those individuals, male and female, who are unable to go maktab or madrasa to learn basic religious knowledge, due to their household, official, or business related activities. Alhamdulillah, many girls have received certification after completing this course, and many of them are benefiting from this course. Numerous batches of this course are successfully going on, and new batches are increasing daily.

1. COURSE FOR ADULT MEN AND WOMEN

Syllabus Includes:

  • Qur'an (Complete Nazirah Qur'an)
  • Hifz-e-Qur'an (last 20 Surahs of the Qur'an e Kareem, Surah Yasin and Ayatul Kursi)
  • Ahadith (Memorization of 40 Ahadith, Everyday Duas, and Sunan).
  • Islamic Knowledge (Seerah, morality, ethics, etc.)
  • Beliefs and Fiqh (Obligatory issues related to Beliefs of Islam, Taharah (Purification), Salah, Zakah, Hajj, Business etc.)
  • Language of the course: (Arabic and Urdu)

2. 40 DAY COURSE

Syllabus Includes:

  • Hifz-e Qur'an with translation and short Tafseer (Last 10 Surah of the Qur'an e Kareem, the first and the last Ruku' of Surah Al-Baqarah and the first and last Ruku of Surah Al Kahf, and Ayatul Kursi)
  • Ahadith (40Ahadith with Masnoon Duas and etiquettes)
  • Seerah and history (120 lessons concerning the biography of the Prophet Muhammad (saws) and 120 lessons concerning the history of the Freedom fight)

3. 20 DAY COURSE

Syllabus Includes:

    • Hifz-e Qur'an with translation and short Tafseer (Last 10 Surah of the Qur'an-e Kareem)
    • Ahadith (10 Masnoon Duas and sunnah related to deeds)
    • Beliefs (Aqaaed)
    • Seerah (Biography of Prophet Muhammad (saws))

4. ONLINE ALIMIYYAT COURSE FOR WOMEN

Specialties of the Course:

  • A nurturing system combined with learning in the light of Islamic guidance.
  • A 6 year Alimiyat Course including complete Dars-e-Nizami Course.
  • Well-trained scholars are here to teach all Dars-e-Nizami Course.
  • Complete surveillance of both the students and teachers, by the management, during the class timing.
  • method of learning and teaching is designed according to the great and famous madarsas.
  • Online, but extremely secure and well organized.
  • Affiliated with great and famous Madaris in both the half-yearly andannual exams.
  • 50 girls have graduated from this course so far.

5. UNDERSTANDING THE QURAN COURSE

Syllabus Includes:

    • The stories of 15 Prophets from the Qur'an.
    • Daily,15 new words and meanings frequently used in Qur'an-e Kareem.
    • Interpretative points regarding the lessons derived from the stories of prophets, may peace be upon them.

6. QURANIC ARABIC GRAMMAR COURSE

Specialties of the Course:

  • Allah ki Iman walon ko Nasihaten (Advices given by Allah the Almighty to the believers) & grammar of Nahw and Sarf.
  • Translation and Tafseer of the verses consisting of the phrase(یا ایہا الذین آمنوا).
  • Grammer of Nahw and Sarf along with Exercises.

7. DAWAH COURSE

Syllabus Includes:

    • A reformative and training article would be sent to the students.
    • They should read it profoundly, and then they will have to present it in a beautiful manner in an online program organized on Sunday so that they could enhance their preaching skills.
