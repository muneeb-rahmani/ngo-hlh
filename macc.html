About Department

MACC

M.A.C.C aims to engage each skilled individuals (men or women) in religious and social services at the level of their locality and towns. A part from the self-protection, they should design an atmosphere of religious, educational and social awareness among the people leading the town and the entire city develop and give the protection for all. Additionally, among the main purposes of MACC is to keep the activities in progress on five bases in every vicinity and town for the protection and development of the Islam and Muslims throughout the country. So that, Muslims could prosper in every aspect of life and Islam could be protected by every means.

Procedure

Organize a program in the locality and encourage the youths and then set up the activities. Make a target of hundred houses and begin with work in a proper manner. Select persons with different skills as a member for these various types of services, to reach the locality and town to the highest point of development.
