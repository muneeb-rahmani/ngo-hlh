About Us

Welcome to Helpline Hyderabad

Helpline Hyderabad Educational and Charitable Trust, is a dynamic welfare and educational organisation with wide ranging aims and objectives. Through this organisation, it is intended to generate religious awareness in the community along with helping the deserving poor, distributing ration to them, assisting in their treatment and facilitating release of innocent youth languishing in jails and conservation of religious precincts etc
