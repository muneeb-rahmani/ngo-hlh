About Department

Wifaqul Makatib

The basic aims and objectives of Idara Wafaq-ul-Makatib include the fact that while Alhamdulillah religious schools are functional in various areas, an educational system for basic religious education ought to be present for students receiving formal education during daytime (whose numbers are multiple times of madrasa students) that has discipline and quality. Hence with this objective and with the assistance of few colleagues, this work began with four Maktabs under the supervision of Hazrat Moulana Shah Jamal-ur-Rahman Sahab (DB) and Alhamdulillah today Maktabs, teachers, workers, students are utilising this system. To make it more effective and to strengthen and spread it, a few issues have been basically determined in consultation with Hazrat Shah Jamal-ur-Rahman Sahab and Alhamdulillah after that it came into existence in September 2013 under the name Wifaq-ul-Makatib.

The System of the Makatib

Alhamdulillah, careful planning and organization are a priority in the establishment of makatib, as unorganized makatib result in inefficient use of teachers and students time.Therefore, a formal syllabus has been designed in addition to a structured teachers' training program. The responsible figures of the makatib are assigned their duties through monthly and quarterly consultations. Additionally, monthly, half-yearly, and annual examinations of each maktab have been designed as a means of regulating and improvingthe standard of education. Five highly-qualified scholars are appointed to supervise the makatib. If you wish to set up an organized maktab in your area, please send your details using the following link. For more details, you may contact these numbers.

Activity of Wifaq


Create awareness in public for establishment of religious schools.


Provide right guidance to people concerned about establishment of Makatib.


Make efforts for the establishment of new organised Maktabs each month.


Take steps to develop educational standards in existing and established schools.


Provide essential goods which required in well-organised Schools and Makatibs.


Conduct male and female workshop (training classes) for teachers teaching in schools..
